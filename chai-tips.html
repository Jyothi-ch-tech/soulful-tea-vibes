How to Prepare Masala Chai ☕

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp black tea leaves
  • 1 inch crushed ginger
  • 2–3 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • Sugar to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil water with spices
  2. Add tea leaves & boil
  3. Add milk and sugar
  4. Simmer 2–3 mins
  5. Strain and enjoy
